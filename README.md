# BIRTHDAY PARTY: Lemonade 🍋

### 🏆 **2ND Place Winner - HackIsland 9.0 Lenovo Spring 2026**

**Lemonade** is an AI-driven web application that empowers creators to build high-impact pitch decks and assists reviewers in evaluating them through an objective, data-backed framework.

#### [visit lemonade](https://lemonadepitcher.vercel.app)  
---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Lemonade is a comprehensive **pitch-refinement ecosystem** designed to bridge the gap between a great idea and a successful presentation.

### 🍋 The Problem

* **Ineffective Pitches:** Roughly 80% of presentations fail to deliver their objective, and 99% of startup pitch decks fail to receive any funding.
* **Complexity:** Aspiring creators often struggle to build effective decks, and existing company trainings can take hours to complete.
* **Reviewer Bottlenecks:** Busy managers are often overwhelmed, leading to delayed reviews and decisions made based on assumptions.

### 💡 Our Solution

**Lemonade** streamlines the pitching process by serving as an intelligent bridge between the **Creator** and the **Reviewer**.

The application is built on three core pillars: **AI-Guided Creation, Video Performance Analysis, and Comparative Review**.

---

### ✨ Key Features & Benefits

| Feature | Description | Benefit to the User |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Guided Pitch Builder** | Continuous interaction between the user and the LLM to collect information. | Walks you through building a perfect pitch without needing hours of training. |
| **PITCH+H Framework** | Grades pitches against a rubric: Premise, Idea, Clarify, Tell, Help, and Heart. | Ensures all critical narrative elements are present for maximum impact. |
| **Review Weights** | Allows reviewers to toggle the importance of specific rubric categories (e.g., weighing 'Heart' over 'Premise'). | Tailors the evaluation to the specific goals of the organization or event. |
| **Video & Speech Analyzer** | Reviews uploaded videos to judge speech, pauses, word count, and tone. | Provides feedback on the 50% of investor attention that relies on oral delivery. |
| **Context-Aware Refinement** | Uses project-specific context and previous drafts to suggest deeper improvements. | Moves beyond generic advice to provide highly specialized technical or creative feedback. |
| **Shareable Review Sessions** | Generates unique links for reviewers to collaborate on evaluations. | Streamlines the decision-making process for hiring committees or judging panels. |
| **Local State Persistence** | Utilizes local storage to save progress, draft states, and session data. | Ensures no progress is lost if a tab is closed or a connection is interrupted. |

By leveraging the **Gemini API**, Lemonade ensures that whether you are launching a fintech disruptor or a community-driven nonprofit, your pitch is tailored, reviewed, and critique viewed to your specific mission.

## ✨ Technical Highlights

* 💎 **Gemini API Integration** — Uses advanced system instructions to focus specifically on pitching excellence.
* 🎥 **Oral Presentation Evaluation** — Creates a transcript of recorded pitches to evaluate speech and delivery.
* 📊 **Dynamic Rubrics** — Reviewers can utilize customizable weights to grade pitches based on specific needs.
* 📄 **Multi-Format Export** — Outputs refined pitch decks, scripts, and summaries ready for use.
* ⚡ **Universal Adaptability** — Scalable platform for any project, from startups to non-profits.
